## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an online service. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information to site administrators.

---

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use Cookies only for essential and functional purposes to ensure the Service operates smoothly. Specifically, we use Cookies to:

- Maintain your session while using the Service.
- Allow basic features such as navigation and page loading.
- Improve the stability and performance of the Service.

We do **not** use Cookies for advertising, tracking, or analytics unless stated otherwise and explicitly consented to.

---

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### **Essential Cookies**
These Cookies are required for the Service to function. Without them, some features may not work correctly.

Examples include:
- Session management cookies  
- Security and authentication cookies  

### **Functional Cookies**
These Cookies help enhance usability, such as remembering your preferences (e.g., language settings).

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party advertising, tracking, or analytics Cookies unless explicitly stated and consented to.

Some third-party service providers (such as hosting or security providers) may use their own Cookies strictly for essential operational purposes.

## 5. Cookie Management

Most web browsers allow you to control Cookies through settings, including:

- Blocking Cookies  
- Deleting stored Cookies

Please note that disabling essential Cookies may impact the functionality of the Service and may prevent you from using certain features.
